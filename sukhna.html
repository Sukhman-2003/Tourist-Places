Sukhna Lake

A Serene Escape in Chandigarh

Nestled at the foothills of the Shivalik range, Sukhna Lake is one of Chandigarh‚Äôs most cherished attractions. This man-made reservoir, spread over 3 km, offers a tranquil retreat for nature lovers, fitness enthusiasts, and tourists alike. The shimmering waters, surrounded by lush greenery, create a mesmerizing atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for morning walks, boating, and birdwatching.
Built in 1958 by Le Corbusier, the visionary architect of Chandigarh, the lake was designed as a sanctuary of peace and relaxation. It is home to migratory birds like Siberian ducks and cranes, enhancing its charm during winter. The well-maintained promenade along the lake is perfect for leisurely strolls, cycling, or simply soaking in the scenic beauty.
