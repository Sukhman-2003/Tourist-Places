Morni Hills

The Only Hill Station Near Chandigarh

Located about 45 km from Chandigarh, Morni Hills is a picturesque hill station in the Shivalik range, offering breathtaking views, lush greenery, and a refreshing climate. It is the only hill station in Haryana, making it a popular getaway for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and trekkers.
Morni Hills is famous for its serene lakes, dense forests, and diverse wildlife, including peacocks, deer, and various bird species. The area is also a hub for adventure activities such as trekking, boating, and rock climbing. The Tikkar Taal Lake, surrounded by hills, is one of the main attractions, perfect for a peaceful retreat.
Whether you're looking for a quiet escape from city life, an adventurous trek, or a scenic drive, Morni Hills offers a refreshing and rejuvenating experience.
