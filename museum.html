Government Museum and Art Gallery

A Treasure Trove of History & Art

Located in the heart of Chandigarh, the Government Museum and Art Gallery is a must-visit destination for history and art enthusiasts. Established in 1968, this museum is renowned for its rich collection of Gandhara sculptures, Pahari and Rajasthani miniature paintings, and artifacts from the Indus Valley Civilization.
Designed by the legendary architect Le Corbusier, the museum is divided into multiple sections, including the Art Gallery, Natural History Museum, Chandigarh Architecture Museum, and National Gallery of Portraits.
