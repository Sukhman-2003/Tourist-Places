Tikkar Taal

A Scenic Twin-Lake Getaway in Morni Hills

Nestled in the Morni Hills, Tikkar Taal is a picturesque twin-lake destination surrounded by lush green hills, offering a peaceful retreat away from the city's hustle and bustle. Located 7 km from Morni village, it is a perfect spot for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and photographers.
