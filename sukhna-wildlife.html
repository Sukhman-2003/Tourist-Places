Sukhna Wildlife Sanctuary

A Hidden Natural Haven in Chandigarht

Located at the foothills of the Shivalik range, Sukhna Wildlife Sanctuary is a pristine natural habitat spread across approximately 26 square kilometers. Situated near the famous Sukhna Lake, this sanctuary is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, making it a paradise for nature lovers, wildlife enthusiasts, and birdwatchers.
Declared a protected area in 1998, the sanctuary is known for its rich biodiversity, including species like sambar deer, barking deer, wild boars, jungle cats, and peacocks. It also serves as a crucial habitat for migratory birds, making it a favored spot for birdwatching.
