Rose Garden of Chandigarh

A Blossoming Paradise of Nature

Located in the heart of Chandigarh, the Rose Garden is the largest of its kind in Asia, and a true testament to the city‚Äôs love for nature and aesthetics. Spanning over 30 acres, it is a vibrant display of blooming flowers, lush greenery, and a tranquil environment. With over 1,600 different varieties of roses, along with various species of fountains, plants, and medicinal herbs, the garden is a serene retreat for nature lovers, photographers, and tourists alike.
The garden, which was established in 1967, is named after the rose‚Äîthe symbol of beauty and love. During the peak blooming season, it becomes a spectacular canvas of colors with thousands of roses in full bloom, attracting flower enthusiasts from around the globe. The garden also hosts the annual Rose Festival, a popular event that celebrates the beauty of flowers with cultural performances, exhibitions, and contests.
Whether you're a flower enthusiast or simply looking for a peaceful escape, the Rose Garden offers a delightful atmosphere, making it a must-visit for anyone exploring the city of Chandigarh.
