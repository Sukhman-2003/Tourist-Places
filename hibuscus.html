Hibiscus Garden

A Floral Wonderland in Chandigarh

Spread across 8 acres in Sector 36, Chandigarh, the Hibiscus Garden is a vibrant botanical paradise dedicated to the enchanting hibiscus flower. Home to over 40 varieties of hibiscus, this garden bursts into a spectacular display of colorful blossoms, offering a serene retreat for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.
Established in 1974, the garden is not just about hibiscus; it also features lush green landscapes, walking trails, and shaded seating areas, making it a perfect spot for morning walks, picnics, and relaxation.
