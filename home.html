A Trove of Fascinating Facts

In Chandigarh's sector numbering system, the number 13 is conspicuously absent due to its inauspicious connotations. This omission was a deliberate coice during the city's planning.
Nek Chand, the visionary behind Rock Graden, initiated its creation secretly in 1957 during his free time.
Chandigarh, a union territory with its own administration, serves as the capital for two Indian states- Punjab and Haryana.
The name 'Chandigarh' is derived from the name of the Hindu goddess Chandi (the warrior incarnation of Goddess Parvati) with the word 'garh' meaning fortress.
